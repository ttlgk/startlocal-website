About Start Local

Founded in 2022 with the mission of supporting small business development in rural communities, Start Local is at home in Alpine Texas. Offering consultancy services to business owners and incubating startups focused on increasing the quality of life for residents in Alpine, Start Local aims to boost hiring prospects and talent retention in the Big Bend.

Start Local is the driving force behind the Granada Theatre, La Sala Studio, Alcove Social, and the Kishmish Plaza.

If you would like to participate in shaping the future of our high desert community, please reach out to collaborate. Whether you are helping us or we are helping you, alignment is key to our joint success.
